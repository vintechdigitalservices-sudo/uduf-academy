Orientation Introduction

Welcome to this orientation session organised by UDUF Africa. It is a great privilege that you are participating in this training.

This orientation is designed to help you understand the meaning, value, and responsibility of volunteering in UDUF Africa. The organisation was established to inspire and connect a new generation of young African leaders who are committed to entrepreneurship, leadership development, community service, and volunteerism.

Before anyone becomes a volunteer or member of UDUF Africa, it is important to clearly understand the vision of the organisation, the expectations from members, and the commitment required to serve effectively.

This session will therefore guide you through the principles of volunteering, the responsibilities expected from members, and the opportunities that come with serving in a purpose driven organisation like UDUF Africa.

It is hoped that this training will help you reflect on your readiness to serve, grow as a leader, and contribute positively to the development of communities and Africa as a whole.
